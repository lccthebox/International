🔥 MISSION · STREET FOOD
Must-try: 납작만두 (flat dumplings) · 칼제비 (hand-torn noodles) · 씨앗호떡 (seed pancake) · 닭강정 (sweet fried chicken). Speak Korean to complete your mission!
5 min walk to Gyesan Cathedral
4:55 PM — 5:30 PM · Stop 2
Gyesan Cathedral & Cheongna Hill
100-year-old Gothic cathedral + the 3.1 Movement staircase & Western missionaries' brick homes. Best photo spot of the day.
ARCHITECTURE · 1902
Inside: stained glass featuring figures in hanbok — a one-of-a-kind blend of East and West. Up the hill: red-brick missionary houses that feel like a different century.
3 min walk to Yi Sang-hwa House
5:35 PM — 6:00 PM · Stop 3
Yi Sang-hwa & Seo Sang-don Houses
Two perfectly preserved hanoks, once home to heroes of Korea's independence movement.
HANOK · HISTORY
Yi Sang-hwa wrote "Even the Stolen Fields Have Spring" — a poem of resistance. Seo Sang-don funded Korea's independence army with his personal fortune. Two legends, side by side.
